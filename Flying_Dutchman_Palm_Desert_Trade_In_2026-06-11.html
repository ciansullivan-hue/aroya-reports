All your TEROS 12 sensors, upgraded to TEROS ONE — for free. AROYA covers 100% of new TEROS ONE hardware. But the terms are zone-based and all-or-nothing:
  • 1 TEROS ONE per zone/valve (not per legacy puck) — Flying Dutchman's flower rooms currently run 2 pucks per zone (Front/Back), so each room collapses to ~4 TEROS ONE instead of 8.
  • 1 Drip & Drain Station per zone at $679 MSRP — paid by the customer, not covered by the promo.
  • AUTOMAIT activated on every zone simultaneously — facility-wide commitment. Can't roll out one room at a time and qualify.
  • Legacy climate-station repeaters (H111/H411 + ATM14) not covered — still need the standard Climate One trade-in.
At MSRP across the full ~61-zone facility, that's $25,559 of free TEROS ONE sensor hardware in exchange for ~$41,419 of Drip & Drain Station purchases + AUTOMAIT subscription. See Scenario D below + aroya.io/terosupgrade. Source: 06.01.2026_AR-Teros-Flyer.pdf.
