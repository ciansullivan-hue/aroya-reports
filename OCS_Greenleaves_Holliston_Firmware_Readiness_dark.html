Recommended firmware-push sequence

  1. Restore the broken radios first (sinks → gateways → climate stations → repeaters → nodes). A radio that isn't on the mesh can't be flashed, and its sensor is dark with it.
  2. Fix the “Probably won't” nodes before you push — reporting but on a critical battery/radio margin; replace batteries / add repeater coverage, confirm recovery, then batch them.
  3. Update the line-powered backbone (climate stations, repeaters, gateways, sinks) — reliable on PoE; get them current before the battery fleet.
  4. Push the “Will sail through” and “Will work” nodes — the bulk; confirm each rejoins.
  5. Return to the “Might work” nodes after their fix.
  6. Restore (or upgrade) offline sensors on otherwise-healthy radios — reseat the TEROS-12 / Atmos-14, or mark it for a TEROS One / Climate One upgrade above.
