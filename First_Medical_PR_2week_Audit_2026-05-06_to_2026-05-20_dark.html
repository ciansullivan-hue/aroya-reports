R&D 6A is a single-room single-HG audit in mid-Bulking, week 5 of 8. Climate is in the Bulking guardrail band on average (VPD 1.35 vs 1.2–1.4; temp 79.9°F vs 75–80°F) but the temperature is riding the upper edge with daily peaks at 82.6°F — beyond Bulking ceiling. Substrate is the bigger story: pore_EC averages 7.39 mS/cm against a 3–7 target band (just over), and is drifting up at +10.8% room-wide over the window, +18.7% on Zone 4 specifically. Overnight dryback is sitting at ~13% — below the 15–25% target for generative Bulking, and Zone 4 is weakest at ~10%. Mesh health is largely fine (every node battery at 100%, gateway online) except Zone 6's two ter15 pair report RSSI 1 and linkQuality 62, materially worse than Zones 2/4 which run RSSI 2–6 and linkQuality 100. The structural finding is that three of six plant zones have no substrate sensors at all — Zones 1, 3, and 5 are flying blind, so the metrics in this audit cover only the even-numbered half of the canopy.
