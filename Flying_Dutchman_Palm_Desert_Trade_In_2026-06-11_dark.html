Estimated values (flagged E in tables — NOT AROYA-published)

  • Legacy device base values used for residual calculation:
    • H32x/H33x/H34x node radio: $280 (estimate)
    • H11x/H41x repeater radio: $320 (estimate)
    • TEROS-12 sensor head: $140 (estimate, Hydrofarm-style)
    • Atmos-14 sensor head: $180 (estimate, Hydrofarm-style)
  • Depreciation: straight-line over 5 years from createdOn. AROYA's published trade-in credits do not depreciate with age, so this affects only the "Est. residual" column — the "Trade credit" column uses the fixed AROYA-published prices regardless.
  • Health haircut: battery 0% → ×0.5 / offline mesh → ×0.4 / retired flag → ×0.2 (floor). One Room-5 device hit the battery=0 haircut.
  • 50% cap on trade-in credit vs new retail: applied per AROYA's general rule. Both the TEROS One and Climate One credits fall well under this cap, so no cap-binding observed.

Warranty rule

  • Everything else: 1 year standard.
