Mesh is healthy across the flower deck

Every one of the 13 active flower rooms reported substrate and climate data on a tight ~1–4 minute cadence for the full 14-day window, with a longest gap of ≤4 minutes in any room. There are no silent nodes and no multi-hour blackouts. This is the reliability signal operators actually feel — no missing data in the AROYA app. One room (Flower 8) logged ~8% fewer substrate readings than its peers, consistent with a single substrate position reporting intermittently; worth a quick probe-seat check but not a fault.

What this view does and does not cover

This dashboard derives reliability from reading cadence and gap analysis over the 14-day window. Per-device battery %, RSSI, link quality and travel time come from the AROYA SPA /devices/ endpoint, which requires a live browser session and was not reachable in this unattended run. For the battery/radio view, re-run with the device-health pipeline against a live SPA tab. Power reminder: only Node and TEROS 12 are battery-powered; Mesh Repeater, Climate One, Atmos 14, EE894, Gateway and Sink are all PoE — never suggest USB-C/wall-wart power for those when troubleshooting an offline device.
