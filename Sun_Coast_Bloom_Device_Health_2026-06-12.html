The read

The mesh is healthy where it’s placed, and dead where it isn’t. All five 100%-coverage rooms (both greenhouses’ HG rooms, 600 R1/R2, GH#1) ran the full 14 days without a gap, and the two Jims Veggies rooms came online cleanly mid-window (06-08 and 06-10 — fresh installs, not faults). The problem is concentrated: 1Z and ‘10 section 2z’ went completely dark around 06-10 after only ~6 days of reporting — 17 devices (nodes + TEROS One heads + the wing’s repeater H3400002795) all at once. Seventeen simultaneous battery deaths is not a thing; an entire wing dropping together is an upstream mesh-path failure — start at the repeater/sink/gateway serving that wing (all PoE — check the PoE drop and switch port, not batteries). Note gateway H2110001144 (Greenhouse #3) also shows no data in the window while its room’s sensors report fine through other paths — verify which sink it’s backhauling through. Separately, 16 devices sit unplaced in inventory including a full gateway + 2 sinks + 6 repeaters: placing one repeater in the 1Z/2z wing would likely harden it permanently. The 1Z probes also read near in-air VWC (~10%) while they were online — re-seat them in active media when the wing comes back.
