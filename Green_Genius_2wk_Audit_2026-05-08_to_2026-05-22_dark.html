The Lead — what the 14 days tell usLa Lectura — lo que dicen los 14 días

Across the 14 days, Green Genius is running irrigation open-loop — no active AROYA harvest groups, no journal entries, no live target ranges. Phase intent has been inferred from the substrate signals: Flower 6 is transitioning into ripening (EC pulling back from a stretch stack ~8.4 toward 5.0 mS/cm, cumulative 14-day dryback 22 pp). The other four rooms read as mid-flower bulking, but with two distinct failure modes: Flower 11 and Flower 10 are vegetatively-pushed (dryback 14–15 pp, the floor of the 15–25 pp band). In Flower 10 — the only climate-instrumented room — the room itself is reinforcing veg steering: VPD mean 0.93 kPa (target 1.2–1.4) and air-temp 73.4 °F (target 75–80 °F). Room 1 and Room 8 are stacking EC, but with severe zone-to-zone uniformity failure: Room 1 Zone 5 shows VWC slope −9 pp/d with EC slope +1.15/d (r² 0.98) — classic channeling. Room 8 Zone 5 pore_EC sits at 3.2 mS while the room runs 4.5 — feed-line anomaly. The single highest-priority action is to diagnose dripper / channeling in Rooms 1 and 8 immediately, then re-establish AROYA harvest groups on every room so the next two-week window can be steered against live targets.

Durante los 14 días, Green Genius opera el riego en lazo abierto — sin grupos de cosecha AROYA activos, sin entradas de diario, sin rangos objetivo en vivo. La intención de fase se ha inferido a partir de las señales del sustrato: Flower 6 está transicionando hacia maduración (CE retrocediendo desde un apilamiento de stretch ~8.4 hacia 5.0 mS/cm, dryback acumulado a 14 días 22 pp). En Flower 10 — la única sala con clima instrumentado — la propia sala refuerza el manejo vegetativo: VPD medio 0.93 kPa (objetivo 1.2–1.4) y temperatura del aire 73.4 °F (objetivo 75–80 °F). Room 1 y Room 8 apilan CE, pero con falla severa de uniformidad zona a zona: Room 1 Zona 5 muestra pendiente VWC −9 pp/d con pendiente CE +1.15/d (r² 0.98) — canalización clásica. Two-shift HVAC framingMarco HVAC de dos turnos

Green Genius runs two photoperiod shifts: rooms on Shift A (Flower 6, Flower 10, Room 8) light at 01:00; rooms on Shift B (Flower 11, Room 1) light at 13:00. This is a deliberate HVAC + electrical load split — at any given hour, only half the facility is in the high-evaporative-demand portion of the day. The climate envelope of Flower 10 (Shift A) cannot be extrapolated to Flower 11 or Room 1 (Shift B) because their lit-window starts when Flower 10 is in P3 / lights-off.

Green Genius opera dos turnos de fotoperíodo: Turno A (Flower 6, Flower 10, Room 8) ilumina a las 01:00; Turno B (Flower 11, Room 1) ilumina a las 13:00. División deliberada de carga HVAC y eléctrica — en cualquier hora dada, solo la mitad de la instalación está en la porción del día de alta demanda evaporativa. La envolvente climática de Flower 10 (Turno A) no puede extrapolarse a Flower 11 o Room 1 (Turno B) porque su ventana iluminada comienza cuando Flower 10 está en P3 / luces apagadas.

4 · Climate ↔ Irrigation Interplay (Flower 10 only)4 · Interacción Clima ↔ Riego (solo Flower 10)

Flower 10 is the only room where both substrate and climate signals coexist, so it's the only room where we can read the full causal chain: climate drives transpiration drives substrate dryback drives EC trajectory. The other four rooms can only show the substrate side of the chain.

Flower 10 es la única sala donde las señales de sustrato y clima coexisten, así que es la única donde podemos leer la cadena causal completa: clima impulsa transpiración impulsa dryback del sustrato impulsa trayectoria de CE.

Both levers point vegetative — irrigation AND climateAmbas palancas apuntan a vegetativo — riego Y clima

In bulking, the room should be pulling hard on the canopy: VPD 1.2–1.4 kPa, air-temp 75–80 °F, daily dryback 15–25 pp, P0 delay 60–90 min. What Flower 10 shows: VPD 0.93 kPa, air-temp 73.4 °F, daily dryback 14.6 pp. The irrigation side is shallow because the climate side under-drives transpiration. The two are reinforcing — neither lever is asking the plant to throw weight. If the grower's intent is bulking, both levers need to move together; tightening irrigation alone (deeper P3 dryback) will starve plants in a low-VPD room. Lift VPD first, watch the dryback follow.

En engorde, la sala debería estar tirando fuerte del dosel: VPD 1.2–1.4 kPa, temp aire 75–80 °F, dryback diario 15–25 pp, retardo P0 60–90 min. Lo que muestra Flower 10: VPD 0.93 kPa, temp 73.4 °F, dryback diario 14.6 pp. El lado del riego es poco profundo porque el lado del clima sub-impulsa la transpiración. Los dos se refuerzan. Si la intención es engorde, ambas palancas deben moverse juntas; apretar solo el riego (P3 más profundo) hará pasar hambre a las plantas en una sala de bajo VPD. Subir el VPD primero.

Channeling diagnostic — Zones 4 & 5Diagnóstico de canalización — Zonas 4 y 5

Zone 5 (82053) shows VWC change −27.1% over the window with EC stacking +1.12 mS·cm⁻¹ and slope +1.15 mS/d at r² 0.98. Zone 4 (82052) shows VWC change −20.9% with EC +0.89 mS·cm⁻¹ slope +0.94/d. Both signatures fit channeling or dripper failure: water is bypassing the plant's root zone, leaving salts to concentrate in a smaller and smaller volume of held substrate. Action: pull the slab and visually inspect the dripper at both zones; measure flow with a graduated cylinder at each emitter; sample runoff EC if any is being collected. If drippers are clogged, replace; if dripper position has shifted, re-stake. Once flow is restored, re-saturate to FC and let the plant pull dryback back to band over 3–5 days.

Zona 5 (82053) muestra cambio VWC −27.1% en la ventana con apilamiento CE +1.12 mS·cm⁻¹ y pendiente +1.15 mS/d con r² 0.98. Zona 4 (82052) cambio VWC −20.9% con CE +0.89 mS·cm⁻¹ pendiente +0.94/d. Ambas firmas encajan con canalización o falla de gotero: el agua esquiva la zona radicular, las sales se concentran en un volumen cada vez más pequeño de sustrato retenido. Acción: sacar el tablón e inspeccionar el gotero en ambas zonas; medir caudal con probeta; muestrear CE de drenaje si se recolecta. Si los goteros están obstruidos, reemplazar; si la posición ha cambiado, re-fijar. Restaurado el caudal, re-saturar a FC y dejar que la planta lleve el dryback a banda en 3–5 días.
